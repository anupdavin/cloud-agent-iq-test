§ 01 The thesis

Businesses don't run on software decisions. We built agents that make them — at the speed of your data.

For twenty years, SaaS asked humans to translate business judgment into forms, dashboards, and tickets. The tools multiplied. The decisions didn't move any faster.

Cloud Agent IQ flips the model. Each agent has a job title, a playbook, and authority to act. You connect your data sources, define guardrails, and the operational work gets done — overnight, at scale, with an audit trail.

This isn't a chatbot. It's a workforce.

§ 03 Architecture

How it actually works.

Four layers. One control plane. Zero "AI magic" handwaving.

  1. 01

    Connect your stack

    OAuth into the tools you already use. 200+ native connectors — CRM, finance, support, warehouse, comms, code. Bring custom APIs via MCP.

  2. 02

    Hire your agents

    Pick from the roster. Each agent comes with a default playbook you can edit in plain English, or rewrite in code if you want precision.

  3. 03

    Set guardrails

    Scope authority, approval thresholds, spend caps, data boundaries. Everything an agent does is logged, diff-able, and revocable.

  4. 04

    Ship. Then tune.

    Agents go live and start producing. Weekly eval reports show what they got right, what they escalated, what to tighten. Humans stay in the loop that matters.

§ 07 Questions, fairly asked

Things people actually want to know.

Is this just GPT with a nicer wrapper?

No. Each agent runs on a purpose-built graph — a typed action layer, memory, an evaluator, and a guardrail engine — on top of the model of your choice (Claude, GPT, Llama, or a private model in your VPC). The model is a component. The system is the product.

What happens when an agent gets something wrong?

Three things. It escalates with full context to the human owner you designated. It files the miss in the weekly eval report. And — depending on severity — it can auto-rollback its own action via a signed reversal. Every action is designed to be undoable.

How long does it take to go live?

Median is 11 minutes for a single agent on our default playbook. Complex deployments (VPC, custom connectors, multi-agent workflows) typically take 2–6 weeks with our solutions team.

Do you train on our data?

Never. Your data stays in your tenant, encrypted with keys you control. We do not train foundation models on customer data, ever. You can review the DPA in the security portal.

Can we build our own agents?

Yes. The Agent SDK (TypeScript and Python) lets you define typed actions, connect arbitrary tools via MCP, and ship a custom agent with the same observability and guardrails as our first-party roster.

What if we want to self-host?

The Enterprise tier supports private cloud deployments into your AWS, GCP, or Azure account. The control plane runs entirely on your infrastructure; model traffic routes to the provider of your choice.
